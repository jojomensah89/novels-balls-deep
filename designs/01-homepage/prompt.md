# Homepage Design Prompt

## Overview

Design a modern, dark-themed homepage for "NovelVerse" - a translated novel reading platform similar to novelbin.com but with a premium, contemporary aesthetic.

## Page Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar (Fixed Top)

- **Logo**: "NovelVerse" text logo on the left
- **Navigation Links**: Home, Browse, Genres (dropdown), Search
- **Search Icon**: Expandable search bar
- **Theme Toggle**: Light/Dark mode switch (optional)
- **Style**: Glassmorphism effect, subtle blur backdrop

### 2. Hero Section

- **Featured Novel**: Large cover image of "Swallowed Star"
- **Title**: Large, bold typography
- **Author**: Smaller text below title
- **Description**: 2-3 lines of novel synopsis
- **CTA Buttons**: "Start Reading" (primary), "Add to Library" (secondary/ghost)
- **Stats**: Chapter count, status badge (Ongoing/Completed)
- **Background**: Gradient overlay on blurred cover image

### 3. Recent Updates Section

- **Section Title**: "Latest Chapters" with "View All" link
- **Layout**: Horizontal scrollable cards or 4-column grid
- **Card Content**:
  - Novel cover thumbnail (small)
  - Novel title (truncated if long)
  - Chapter number + title
  - Time ago badge (e.g., "2 hours ago")
- **Hover Effect**: Subtle lift/scale animation

### 4. Popular Novels Section

- **Section Title**: "Trending Now" or "Popular This Week"
- **Layout**: 5-6 novel cards in a row
- **Card Content**:
  - Cover image (portrait ratio, ~150x200px)
  - Title below image
  - Genre tags (1-2 small badges)
  - Rating stars (optional)
- **Ranking Numbers**: Optional "1, 2, 3..." overlay on top novels

### 5. Genre Quick Access

- **Section Title**: "Browse by Genre"
- **Layout**: Grid of genre cards (3x3 or horizontal scroll)
- **Genres**: Action, Romance, Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Adventure, Martial Arts, Comedy, Drama, Horror
- **Card Style**: Icon + genre name, gradient background per genre
- **Hover**: Highlight effect

### 6. Footer

- **Links**: About, Contact, DMCA, Privacy Policy
- **Social Icons**: Optional
- **Copyright**: "Â© 2024 NovelVerse"
- **Style**: Minimal, dark background

## Visual Style

### Colors

- **Background**: Deep dark (#0a0a0f or similar)
- **Cards**: Slightly lighter (#12121a)
- **Primary Accent**: Purple/Violet (#8b5cf6)
- **Secondary**: Blue (#3b82f6)
- **Text**: White (#ffffff) and gray (#a1a1aa)
- **Gradients**: Purple to blue gradients for CTAs

### Typography

- **Headings**: Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Outfit, or similar)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (16px base)
- **Novel Titles**: Semi-bold

### Effects

- **Cards**: Subtle border (1px semi-transparent)
- **Hover States**: Smooth transitions (0.2s)
- **Shadows**: Soft, colored shadows on hover
- **glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effect on nav and overlays

## Responsive Notes

- Mobile: Stack sections vertically, hamburger menu
- Cards become horizontal scrollable on mobile
- Hero section adapts to smaller cover + stacked content

## Reference Sites

- novelbin.com (layout inspiration)
- novelhi.me (clean reading interface)
- Crunchyroll/Netflix (modern card design)
